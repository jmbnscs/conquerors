About

Get to know our journey and what we are up to at the moment.

  • June 2019

    Where We Started

    Alfredo, Nori, JM, and KL first met each other inside the tiny hot room of PUP Main's 4th floor East wing. It took them a while before they actually became friends and from then on, they navigated college life together, through thick and thin.

  • September 2021

    The Conquerors is Born

    After being separated during their sophomore year, the four reunited and became classmates again on their junior year. As expected, they became groupmates in almost every groupings and finally made their team's name official when they were working on an activity for their Project Management subject.

  • 2021 - 2022

    Various Projects

    Below is the list of projects they have worked on together:

    PUPSIS Concerns (2021) - Developed a complaint management system for PUPSIS.

    PILIpinas (2021) - Made a business plan for a filipino-centric shopping app on their Technopreneurship subject.

    Hotel Del Luna (2022) - Created a console application that manages hotel reservations.

  • August 2022 - Present

    Capstone Project

    Currently, the team is working on their Capstone Project which is a Billing Management System for GS-Tech, a small internet service provider in Pasig.

Project Leader
